## How It Works

### Step 1: Submit Your Resume
Choose one of two methods:
- **Upload PDF**: Click "Upload Resume" and select your PDF file
- **Paste Text**: Copy your resume content into the text area

### Step 2: (Optional) Add Target Information
- **Target Role**: Specify a job you're interested in (e.g., "Software Engineer")
- **Job Description**: Paste a specific job posting to get tailored analysis

### Step 3: Get Instant Analysis
The system processes your information and provides:
- Your identified skills and experience level
- 3 career role suggestions with match percentages
- Visual radar chart of your competencies
- Skill gaps for each suggested role
- Personalized learning recommendations
- ATS optimization feedback

## Understanding Your Results

### Match Percentages
- **75-100%**: Excellent fit - well-positioned for senior roles
- **60-74%**: Strong fit - ready for mid-level positions with some upskilling
- **40-59%**: Solid fit - clear growth path with focused learning
- **Below 40%**: Developing - focus on building core competencies

### Radar Chart
- **Blue Line**: Your current skill levels
- **Orange Line**: Industry standard benchmarks

### Priority Levels

## Tips for Best Results

### Resume Quality
- Use clear, standard section headings (Experience, Skills, Education)
- Include quantifiable achievements (e.g., "Increased sales by 25%")
- List specific technologies and tools you've used
- Ensure contact information is complete

### Analysis Accuracy
- Provide complete work history for better skill detection
- Include both technical and soft skills
- Be specific about projects and responsibilities
- Use industry-standard terminology

### Career Planning
- Focus on high-priority skill gaps first
- Consider multiple career paths, not just one
- Balance short-term goals with long-term vision
- Regularly update your analysis as you gain new skills
